Search can be limited to a specific volume with the meta clause, e.g. meta vol=i/X or meta vol=I/X limits the search to the first volume while meta orig=pdf limits the search to the 5 digitally-born volumes.

{% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}

The within clause can be used to limit the search to the so called sections: front (the frontmatter), intro (the prefaces in the first volume), list (the list of entries provided at the beginning of every volume), body (the entries), errata (corrections and additions; this section is contained in body), back (backmatter), inset (loose insets with the lists of sources and abbreviations).
