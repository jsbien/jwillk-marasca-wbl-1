Search can be limited to a specific volume with the within clause, e.g. within vol6 or within vol6part2. The clause can be also used to limit the search to the following sections: body (entries and their corrections), errata (just the corrections), intro (introductory texts), info (other informations), varia (dedications, subscriber lists), front (frontmatter), back (backmatter).
